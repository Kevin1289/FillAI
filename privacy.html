FillAI Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

FillAI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information Collection

Information We Collect:

  • Anonymous usage statistics (number of forms filled, success rates)
  • Technical information for debugging (no personal data)
  • Form field labels and context (only when actively using the extension)

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • Form contents or submissions
  • Personal information
  • Browsing history
  • API keys (stored locally in your browser only)

Data Storage and Security

  • All user settings and history are stored locally in your browser
  • API keys are stored locally and never transmitted to our servers
  • Usage statistics are anonymized and stored securely
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect any collected data

Third-Party Services

Google Gemini AI Service

FillAI uses Google's Gemini AI to generate form responses. When using our extension:

  • Form field data is sent to Gemini AI to generate appropriate responses
  • This data processing is governed by Google's privacy policies and terms
  • We recommend reviewing:
    • Gemini API Terms of Service
    • Gemini Privacy & Terms

Data Sharing

We share data with the following parties:

  • Google Gemini AI - for processing form field data and generating responses
  • We DO NOT share your data with any other third parties

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your local data stored by the extension
  • Clear your local data through the extension settings
  • Opt-out of anonymous usage statistics
  • Uninstall the extension at any time

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store support channel.
